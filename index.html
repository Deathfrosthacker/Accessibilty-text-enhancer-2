About A11yText Enhancer

This tool helps make text more readable for people with dyslexia, ADHD, low vision, and other reading differences.

  • Lexend font — Designed specifically for cognitive readability
  • Bionic Reading — Guides the eye by highlighting word stems
  • Full customization — Because every reader is different

All processing happens in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.
